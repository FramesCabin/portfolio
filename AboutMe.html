WHO IS
FRAMES CABIN?

My name is Marcos Cabaña Tissot (a marco in Spanish is a frame and a cabaña is a cabin, hence the name).

I am a 24 years old Spanish-Argentinian guy studying "Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence MSc" at DTU.

Passionate about art and storytelling, I initially thought engineering was incompatible with animation. Now I know it is possible to tell amazing stories with the engineering hat on, and it is called: VIDEOGAMES!

I am excited to apply to DADIU so I can learn not only how to “code” to create a videogame but also to work in such a diverse and cross-disciplinary team of students.
